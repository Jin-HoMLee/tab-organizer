How to import into Notion

  1. Click one of the Export buttons above to download a file of your open tabs.
  2. Go to Notion and open or create a page where you want to import your tabs.
  3. For Markdown: Drag and drop the .md file into Notion, or copy/paste its contents.
    For HTML: Open the .html file in your browser, select the list, and copy/paste it into Notion.
  4. Notion will create a list of links to your tabs.
